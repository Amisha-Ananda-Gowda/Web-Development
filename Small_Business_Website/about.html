about us

We Bake Every Item From The Core Of Our Hearts

Welcome to Bakery, a local bakery with a passion for baking. Since 1947, we've been serving our community in Golihole with freshly made, handcrafted delights. From artisanal bread to custom cakes, our commitment to quality and flavor is at the heart of everything we do.

Our team of dedicated bakers and friendly staff take pride in being part of your life's sweet moments. Whether you're dropping by for a daily treat or planning a special celebration, Bakery is here to make every visit a delightful experience. Join us in savoring the joy of baking.

Our Mission

"At Bakery, our mission is to infuse every bite with the pure essence of joy. We're more than just a bakery; we're a devoted team of enthusiastic bakers and creators. We firmly believe in the extraordinary power of exceptional food to forge connections and bring people together. Our dedication goes beyond the ovens; it extends to the heart of our community, where we strive to create unforgettable moments with each pastry, cake, and loaf of bread. Our commitment is to craft exceptional experiences, one delectable creation at a time."
